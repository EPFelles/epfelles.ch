Our goals

Our goals are simple, we strive to create a community of female students and doctoral students at EPFL, to facilitate the integration and support them in their student life.

What we do

We organize study sessions, aperitifs and outings, both professional and social, as well as conferences, workshops and information sessions in coordination with companies and other associations. We try to promote female role-models in science, engineering and architecture, both EPFL and external. Do you have any ideas for what our next event should be? Don’t hesitate to contact us!

EPFelles Podcast

Our podcast is up! This is so exciting for us, we can’t wait to see what you think.

In weekly 20 minutes episodes, we'll share information and personal experience about a series of subjects, spanning from relationships, health, great women in science, and many more. Sometimes there will be experts joining us and answering your questions.

Enjoy the podcast on Spotify and on RSS

Do not hesitate to contact us if you have any remarks or ideas for the next episodes.

Sexism and harassment on campus

We support Polyquity's initiative to fight against sexism, harassment, homophobia and sexual assaults.
