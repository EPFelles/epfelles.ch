EPFellesAssociation

In French, the letter "L" is pronounced as "elles" meaning plural "she", this is our way of reclaiming our space in EPFL! The goals of our association are:

  • Creating a community of women students at EPFL
  • Facilitate integration of women students and provide support during their studies
  • Promote women of EPFL and women role models in science, engineering and architecture
  • Share information and services dedicated to women students at EPFL
