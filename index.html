Our goals

Our goals are simple, we strive to create a community of female students and doctoral students at EPFL, to facilitate the integration and support them in their student life.

What we do

We organize study sessions, aperitifs and outings, both professional and social, as well as conferences, workshops and information sessions in coordination with companies and other associations.
